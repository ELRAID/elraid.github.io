Discover About Us!

We are tech enthusiast met at a MSc program in NLP.
We first learned first hand the problem of resource while we pursue a research work in machine translation, computer vision and text understanding.
We live in Ethiopia, a country with more than 80 spoken languages and all of these are under-resourced with no standard data set, text processing tools and benchmark experiment.

Under-Resourced Languages(URLs) as suggested by the name lucks basic resources to enable a any kind of researches. The basic resource required for NLP research such as Morphological Analyzer, POS tagger, Tree Bank and Stemmer are only available for one language or are non-existence. And even there exist a any, the existing tools are either poorly designed or covers a limited functionality. Therefore, and researchers such as ours often-times spent d a significant percent of their time on a manual data preparation task instead of focusing on their main research challenge.
